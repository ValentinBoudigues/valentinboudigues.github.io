Robocup SSL

IA made for SSL robots

This project was created by a large group of friend

We developed an IA to participate at the French Robocup 2022 at Bordeaux, in which we took 1rst place and European Robocup 2022 at Guimarães in Portugal, in which in took 2nd place

For this project, we have coded it entirely in Python

We used vector, cartesians and polar coordinates, and much more
